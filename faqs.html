Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know before ordering ♡

Ordering & Customisation

Can I customise the colour or size of a piece?

Yes! Almost everything on the shop can be customised. You can choose colours at checkout, and if you have something specific in mind, just tick the customisation box and I'll reach out to discuss the details. Keep in mind that custom requests may have an additional charge depending on the complexity.

How do I place an order?

Add your items to the cart, then click "Order via WhatsApp." Your order summary will be sent directly to me and I'll confirm availability, payment details, and your delivery address from there.

Can I order something that isn't listed on the website?

Absolutely! I take fully custom commissions. Message me on WhatsApp or Instagram with a reference image or description and I'll give you a quote and timeline.

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum — you can order a single Rs. 800 keychain if you'd like. Every order gets the same love regardless of size.

Production & Delivery

How long does it take to make my order?

All pieces are made to order. Standard production time is 5–7 days before dispatch. Complex or custom orders may take a little longer — I'll always let you know upfront.

Do you deliver across Pakistan?

Yes! I deliver nationwide via courier. Delivery usually takes 2–4 working days after dispatch depending on your city. Karachi orders are sometimes faster.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery is Rs. 300 flat. Orders above Rs. 5,000 qualify for free shipping — the promo code YARNIA also gives you 10% off your first order.

How will my order be packed?

Orders are packed carefully to protect the piece during transit. Gift-wrapping or a handwritten note can be added — just mention it when you message me on WhatsApp.

Product Care

How do I clean my crochet piece?

Spot-clean with a damp cloth and mild soap for small marks. For a full wash, hand-wash gently in cold water and lay flat to dry. Do not machine wash or tumble dry.

My piece looks slightly different from the photo — is that normal?

Yes, and it's a feature! Every piece is entirely handmade, so slight variations in colour, size, and stitch tension are natural. No two pieces are identical — yours is genuinely one of a kind.

Are the materials safe for children?

The yarn and stuffing I use are soft and non-toxic, but small pieces like keychains may not be suitable for very young children. If you're buying for a toddler, message me and I can advise on the safest options.

Returns & Issues

What if my order arrives damaged?

Please message me on WhatsApp with a photo within 48 hours of receiving your order and I'll sort it out — either a replacement or a refund, depending on the situation.

Can I cancel or change my order?

Changes and cancellations are possible if I haven't started making your piece yet. Message me as soon as possible. Once production has begun, changes may not be possible.
