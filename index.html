About Yarnia

Yarnia started as a late-night hobby and turned into something we never expected — a small shop where every order is made by hand, with care, and with intention.

We're based in Karachi, Pakistan. Every bunny, flower, and character is crocheted from scratch — no machines, no factories, no two pieces exactly alike.

We believe handmade things carry something mass-produced things never can: the time, the patience, and the love of the person who made it. That's what you get with every Yarnia piece.

Custom orders are always welcome. If you have something specific in mind — a colour, a character, a size — reach out on WhatsApp or Instagram and we'll make it happen.
