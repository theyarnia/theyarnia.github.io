The hands behind Yarnia

No factories, no shortcuts. Just my hands, some yarn, and a little too much love.

I started Yarnia right here in Karachi as a creative escape, and today it’s a tiny, one-person shop dedicated to the art of handmade crochet. From squishy animals to custom bags and flowers that stay fresh forever, every single piece is stitched from scratch — by me.

When you buy from me, you’re getting a unique creation that took hours of quiet patience to make. Slight wobbles and one-of-a-kind quirks aren’t flaws; they’re the proof a human made it.
